I'm the developer of that package. It's a standalone launcher that allows to manage several versions of vvvv, a graphical programming environment for .NET.
You can see it's being actively maintained and used by many people in the bug tracker (https://github.com/sebescudie/GammaLauncher/issues) and often mentioned in the vvvv forums (https://forum.vvvv.org/search?q=gammalauncher).
